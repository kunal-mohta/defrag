DEFRAG 2018
Do you want to learn from and have the chance to pick the brains of the best computer scientists in the country and the world? Do terms like Computer Vision, Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain and Privacy challenges excite you? This APOGEE we invite you to DeFrag, the flagship technical lecture series of APOGEE, where distinguished speakers from various domains of computing give talks on their areas of expertise which are at the very threshold of Computer Science today, including but not limited to the ones mentioned above. The talks will be followed by an interactive Q and A session as well. Be there to broaden your computing horizons and learn from the best in the business!
#Speaker 1
Dr. David G Stork
A Distinguished Research Scientist and Research Director at Rambus Labs, this MIT graduate in Physics holds over 40 US Patents and his books on Pattern Classification, Optics, and Computer Vision are in use in over 290 universities worldwide. He has also held faculty positions in the academic departments of Physics, Mathematics, Electrical Engineering, Neuroscience, Psychology and Art and Art History at Clark, Boston and Stanford Universities.
#Speaker 2
Mr. Abhishek Syal
A BITS Pilani 2010 Electronics and Instrumentation graduate with a Master’s in Business Administration from the prestigious MIT Sloan School of Management, he is one of the MIT TR 35 under 35 innovators and the finalist of IET PATW. He is the CEO of ARISE Impact, a start-up that creates self-learning resources for the differently abled.
#Speaker 3
Dr. Neena Gupta
​​​An Indian mathematician, she has solved a Mathematics problem that baffled mathematicians around the world for 70 years. She is also the recipient of the Ramanujan Prize (2014). The Indian National Science Academy, while awarding her the Medal for Young Scientists, described her research as ‘one of the best works in algebraic geometry in recent years done anywhere in the world.''
#Speaker 4
Dr. Sachin P Lodha
​A Computer Science graduate from IIT Mumbai with a Ph. D in Computer Science from Rutgers University, New Jersey, USA, he is a Principal Scientist at TCS Innovation Labs. He heads the Applied Algorithm Group which actively works on problems related to data center optimization and enterprise crowdsourcing. ​
#Speaker 5
Dr. Ponnurangam Kumaraguru
A Visiting Researcher at the Data Security Council of India with a Ph. D from School of Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University, he is currently the Associate Professor, Computer Science at IIIT – D. He is a Co=Principle Investigator in a Project approved at the Europe Union FP7 which is about 5.3 million Euros. His research interests include privacy, e-crime, and usable security.
